Accommodations: From Hotels

ejudo Island features a wide variety of accommodations ranging from luxurious hotels to local hostels.
If you would like to experience Jeju’s culture, you can either stay at a local lodging or a guest house where you can meet other travelers and local hoteliers.
When choosing your accommodation, keep an eye out for Good Price Businesses that have been verified through a strict process and “Goodstay” residences.
